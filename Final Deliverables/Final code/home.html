Detect if your plant is infected!!!

Agriculture is one of the major sectors world wide. Over the years it has developed and the use of technologies and equipment replaced almost all the traditional methods of farming. The plant disease affects the production. Identification of the disease and taking necessary precautions is all done through naked eye, which requires labours and laboratories. This application helps farmers in detecting the disease by observing the spots in the leaves, which in turn saves effort and labour cost.
